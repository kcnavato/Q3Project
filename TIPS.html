Tips for Fire Prevention

  • Install smoke detectors in every room and test them regularly
  • Have a fire extinguisher readily available and know how to use it
  • Keep flammable materials away from heat sources
  • Don't leave cooking food unattended
  • Never smoke inside
  • Have a fire escape plan in place and practice it regularly

What to do in Case of a Fire

If a fire does occur, it's important to act quickly and calmly. Remember the following:

  1. Call the fire department and alert others in the building
  2. Exit the building immediately, using the designated fire escape route if possible
  3. If there's smoke, stay low to the ground
  4. If your clothes catch on fire, stop, drop, and roll
  5. Don't go back into the building until the fire department has deemed it safe to do so
